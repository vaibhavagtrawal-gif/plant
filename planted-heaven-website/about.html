Our Story

Where Nature Meets Nurture

Born from a love for nature and a vision to reconnect people with the earth, Planted Heaven was founded to make green living more accessible, aesthetic, and meaningful.

We are a passionate, plant-loving company dedicated to bringing the calming beauty of greenery into everyday spaces. With a deep-rooted belief in the power of plants to heal, transform, and elevate, we curate lush green experiences for homes, offices, events, and more.

Whether it's a tiny desk succulent or an entire indoor jungle, we believe that plants have the power to turn any space into a sanctuary.
